Introduction

Dominion Bible School (DBS) is a Bible and leadership Training Centre designed to equip kingdom minded believers for ministry. We believe that effectiveness in ministry can only happen when believers are equipped. DBS is on a mission to raise men and women who are ready to take the world for Christ. Our facilitators are carefully selected, they are among the finest teachers of the word in the body of Christ today with accumulated decades of experience in ministry. Our course modules are excellently packaged providing unique approach to teaching of God's word and practical hands-on ministry experience to learning through impartation of biblical knowledge and wisdom in raising kingdom disciples with maturity and understanding of the way of the kingdom. I welcome you to a unique platform of opportunities, impartation, biblical exegesis, insightful teaching and transformation.
